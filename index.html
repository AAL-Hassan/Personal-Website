Portfolio

Future-Past

Future-Past is a turn-based, side-scrolling Platformer/RPG developed in Unity using C#. The player assumes control of a character capable of navigating left, right, and jumping within the overworld. Throughout the overworld, the player may encounter enemies that trigger turn-based battles reminiscent of gameplay found in titles such as Pokémon and Final Fantasy. The objective of the player is to successfully navigate through three levels to reach the conclusion of the game.

GameSwap

GameSwap is a simulated online video game store that was created utilising fundamental HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Payment processing is facilitated through the utilisation of the payment platform, Stripe. Users have the ability to select a specific game to view its product page and corresponding price. Upon selecting a game, users can proceed to click the purchase button, which subsequently redirects them to a Stripe checkout page for the selected product.

WebPulse

WebPulse serves as the official online platform of TheWebPulse, providing a website building service that facilitates connections between clients and proficient web developers. This website incorporates interactive elements, such as videos and carousels, utilizing the swiper CSS library. Moreover, it employs the Bootstrap and Ress CSS libraries for the purpose of designing and styling the website.

GPU Metrics

GPU Metrics is a platform dedicated to GPU benchmarking, allowing users to compare various GPUs across a range of games. The site boasts a minimalist and user-friendly design. The charts utilised on the platform are created using the chart.js library, which has been customised to better suit the specific requirements of the site. These modifications streamline the process of inputting new data values into the array, ensuring they are automatically arranged in descending order regardless of their original placement. Additionally, the charts feature auto-alternating bar colours, improving readability for users.
